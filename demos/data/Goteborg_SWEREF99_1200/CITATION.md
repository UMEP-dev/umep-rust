# Gothenburg (Kronenhuset) Demo Data

## Site

Kronenhuset courtyard, central Gothenburg, Sweden (57.7 N, 12.0 E).
1 m resolution, EPSG:3007 (SWEREF99 12 00), 234 x 223 pixels.

## Data

DSM, DEM, CDSM, and land cover rasters originate from the UMEP project
and were used in the original SOLWEIG validation study. The field
measurement CSV (`tests/validation/kronenhuset/measurements_kr.csv`)
contains hourly radiation budget observations from October 7, 2005.

## Citation

Lindberg, F., Holmer, B. & Thorsson, S. (2008). SOLWEIG 1.0 -- Modelling
spatial variations of 3D radiant fluxes and mean radiant temperature in
complex urban settings. *International Journal of Biometeorology*, 52,
697--713. doi:[10.1007/s00484-008-0162-7](https://doi.org/10.1007/s00484-008-0162-7)

services. *Environmental Modelling & Software*, 99, 70--87.
doi:[10.1016/j.envsoft.2017.09.020](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.envsoft.2017.09.020)
